Things to Do

Island Activities

From thrilling adventures to serene relaxation, discover the diverse experiences that await in El Nido.

Island Hopping Tours

Explore the famous island hopping routes (A, B, C, D) to discover hidden lagoons, secret beaches, and stunning viewpoints.

Beach Relaxation

Unwind on El Nido's most beautiful beaches including Nacpan, Lio, and Las Cabanas with their pristine white sand and turquoise waters.

Snorkeling & Diving

Discover vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life in some of the world's most biodiverse waters.

Kayaking Adventures

Paddle through hidden lagoons and mangrove forests to discover secluded spots inaccessible by boat.

Sunset Viewing

Witness spectacular sunsets from Las Cabanas Beach or our rooftop terrace with a refreshing cocktail in hand.

El Nido Nightlife

Experience the vibrant local nightlife with beachfront bars, fire dancers, and live music just steps from our inn.
